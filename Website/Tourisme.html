Whale Watching


Price: $80.99

A beautiful place in eastern town of Quebec, River-Du-Loup that's where we take the luxury cruise to see Whale watching. The cruise trip costs about $35, and it's a one hour ride. Then we head back to Montreal in the afternoon.



Lavender Garden


Price: $25

A beautiful place at Fitch Bay that is where the Blue Lavender garden is located. A field with 300,000lavender plants. There will be a guided tour which allows you to discover the interpretive center, watch a short film on the history of Blue Lavender and to walk through the beautiful fields. Then we head back to Montreal in the evening.

Granby Zoo


Price: $9.99

The Granby Zoo is located in Granby, Quebec and is one of the Quebec's major tourist attractions. There are animals from Africa, Asia, and South America. There is a water park in adjacent side of the zoo. Then we head back to Montreal in the evening.



Mont-Tremblant


Price: $39.99

We will arrive to Saint-Saveur Village where we can see the main street which becomes alive with the animation. Then, we take the 50 minutes cruise on the beautiful Lac des Sables in Saint Agathe des Monts, where we get to see the beautiful view of the seven island. After lunch, we will head to Mont-Tremblant village and take the cable car to see the spledid view. Then we head back to Montreal in the afternoon.
